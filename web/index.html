CORS Proxy (Cloudflare Worker)

This tool uses a private Cloudflare Worker to bypass CORS restrictions and securely fetch Polarsteps data. It processes your requests directly and does not store or track your data.

Your data remains private and is only used for generating the GPX export. The worker code source is available in this repository.
